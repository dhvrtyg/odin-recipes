Turmeric Milk

Turmeric milk is a great home remedy for cough, congestion, colds, and skin problems. This winter, replace your usual hot cocoa with an invigorating cup of wellness â€” bonus points for jammies and slippers. Turmeric can stain clothing and countertops, so wear an apron and proceed with caution.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pnch ground ginger
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 1 pinch ground allspice
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 teaspoon honey, or to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Whisk together turmeric, cardamom, black pepper, ginger, cloves, and allspice in a small bowl; set aside
  2. Heat milk in a small saucepan over medium heat until heated through, 3 to 4 minutes; stir honey and vanilla into milk until completely dissolved
  3. Whisk 1 teaspoon turmeric mixture inot milk; reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until flavors blend, 2 to 3 minutes. Pour mixture through a stainer.
